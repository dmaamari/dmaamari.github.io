Methodology

The aim of this fieldwork survey was to gather socioeconomic data and identify materials for different construction methods and material flows into and out of the city. We used the concept of Urban Metabolism to calculate the material and energy requirements of one individual in the growing, formerly rural area. These estimates allowed the city officials to make informed decisions on the material requirements associated with the cityâ€™s new policies for urban expansion.
In March 2019, we surveyed a representative street of Afriquia 2 district in Ben Guerir, Morocco, to determine the material composition and properties of a typical house in this district. Sample size was 40 residential and mixed-use buildings. We used Fulcrum software for data gathering and Tableau & Excel for Data synthesis.
